About IndTec Expo 2026

IndTec Expo 2026 is India’s premier platform showcasing cutting-edge industrial technologies, automation, machinery, tools, and next-gen manufacturing innovations. The expo brings together industry leaders, manufacturers, suppliers, engineers, and decision-makers under one roof.The event serves as a powerful hub for technology exchange, product demonstrations, and the knowledge sharing. With a strong focus on Industry 4.0, smart manufacturing, robotics, material handling, digital production systems, and sustainable industrial solutions, the expo empowers companies to accelerate growth and stay competitive in a rapidly to the evolving market.Visitors can experience live machinery demos, discover breakthrough technologies, interact with global brands, attend technical sessions, and unlock the new sourcing of opportunities. Whether you're a buyer, manufacturer, trader, distributor, industrial consultant, or technology provider, IndTec Expo 2026 offers unmatched opportunities to connect, collaborate, and scale innovation across multiple sectors.

Frequently Asked Question

Discover why IND-TEC stands as India’s most influential industrial technology exhibition.

IND-TEC 2025 focuses on large, small, and medium enterprises across a wide spectrum of industries including Engineering, Robotics, Machine Tools, Electricals & Electronics, Instrumentation & Automation, Fire & Safety, Digital Technology, Welding Processes, and Equipment Manufacturing.

IND-TEC 2025 marks its 7th edition and is the largest show along the Tamil Nadu–Karnataka border. The expo reaches key industrial cities including: Bangalore, Hosur, Krishnagiri, Salem, Vellore, Hosakote, Sriperumbudur, Chennai, Erode, Madurai, Coimbatore, Tirupur and more.

IND-TEC is the only exhibition offering businesses the ability to reach a diverse, pan-India audience. Exhibitors also get the opportunity to promote offerings regionally based on targeted business needs.

The expo provides a dynamic platform for companies to exhibit products, explore partnerships, form business tie-ups, and showcase unique industrial solutions powered by the latest advancements in design, engineering, equipment, supplies and support services. It is also an ideal venue for launching new products & services.

IND-TEC is dedicated to supporting SMEs by boosting business development, enhancing market visibility, and improving profitability. The expo strives to uplift small and medium industries by giving them a strong platform for networking and growth.

IndTec Expo 2026 is India’s premier technology innovation showcase, uniting industry leaders, manufacturers, engineers, innovators, and decision-makers under one roof.
